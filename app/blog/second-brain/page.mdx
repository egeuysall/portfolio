# Spot v1.0 is Live

Most event apps suck. They’re slow, bloated, and throw a bunch of random events at you.

I wanted something cleaner. Something that actually feels useful and fast.

So I built Spot.

## What Spot Does

**Spot is a personal event discovery app.** You answer a few questions and it shows you events that match your vibe.

No accounts. No digging through filters. Just relevant stuff that actually makes sense for you.

## How It Works

Spot uses real-time data from **Ticketmaster** and runs your answers through **OpenAI** to figure out your style.

The frontend is built with **Next.js 15**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **TypeScript**. It’s fast, responsive, and works great on mobile too.

## What’s Coming Next

This is just version 1.

I’m planning to add saved preferences, smarter filters, and interactive features like RSVPs and event ratings.
